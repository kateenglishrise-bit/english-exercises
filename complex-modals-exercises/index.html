Need help? Quick Grammar Rules Cheat Sheet (Rules 1 to 7)
Rule 1 (Future/Ongoing Possibility): May / might / could + have + V3 May / might / could + be + V-ing
Rule 2 (Past Continuous Possibility): May / might / could + have been + V-ing
Rule 3 (Hypothetical Past): Would / will + have + V3
Rule 4 (Past Regret / Criticism): Should / ought to + have + V3
Rule 5 (Negative Deduction): Can't / couldn't + have + V3
Rule 6 & 7 (Positive Deduction & Necessity): Must + have + V3 (Certain past) Must have (had) to + Verb (Obligation)
