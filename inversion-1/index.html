Ex 1

Choose the correct option

1. _______ have I heard such a ridiculous excuse.

2. _______ is she a great singer, but she also plays the piano.

3. _______ should passengers open the doors while the train is moving.

Ex 2

Type the correct inversion

Type the correct auxiliary verb and subject to complete the inversion. Pay attention to the tense required by the words in brackets!

1. Seldom (we / see) such an amazing performance in this theater.

2. Not only (he / win) the gold medal, but he also broke the world record.

3. In no way (this / be) your fault. Please don't blame yourself.

4. Rarely (I / have) felt so completely exhausted after a hike.

5. Little (she / expect) to see her childhood friend at the conference.

6. Under no circumstances (you / should) give out your password to anyone online.

7. Hardly (we / had / started) eating when the fire alarm went off.

8. Never before (I / have / witnessed) such a display of arrogance.

9. Only later (they / did / discover) that the painting was a forgery.

10. Not until the next morning (she / did / realise) how serious the situation was.
